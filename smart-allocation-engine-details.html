Overview

The Smart Allocation Maximizer solves the complex challenge of distributing products across multiple channels, locations, and customer segments for maximum profitability. It dynamically balances inventory across retail stores, e-commerce fulfillment centers, wholesale channels, and direct-to-consumer operations. By considering factors like demand velocity, margin profiles, shipping costs, and service level requirements, it ensures the right products are in the right places at the right time. This AOM typically delivers 432% improvement in allocated quantities while reducing stockouts and overstock situations simultaneously.

Key Features

  • Dynamic inventory allocation based on real-time demand signals
  • Multi-warehouse optimization with cross-docking capabilities
  • Intelligent routing based on shipping costs and delivery times
  • Automated rebalancing to prevent stockouts and overstock
  • Integration with WMS, TMS, and order management systems
