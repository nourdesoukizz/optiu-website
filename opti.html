What is Opti?

Opti is your conversational interface to the world of optimization. Just like ChatGPT makes AI accessible through conversation, Opti makes complex optimization accessible to everyone.

You:
"Optimize my vendor mix under $2M budget"
Opti:
Based on your $2M budget, I recommend: 60% Vendor A ($1.2M), 30% Vendor B ($600K), 10% Vendor C ($200K). This allocation maximizes cost efficiency while minimizing supply chain risk.
