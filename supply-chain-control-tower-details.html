Overview

The PO Lead Time Injector brings intelligence to supplier performance management by accurately predicting when orders will actually arrive, not just when they're promised. Using historical performance data, current capacity constraints, seasonal patterns, and even external factors like port congestion or weather delays, it provides realistic delivery windows that operations teams can trust. By reducing uncertainty in lead times by 4+ days, it enables better production planning, reduces expediting costs, and improves customer delivery promises. This AOM is essential for companies managing complex global supply chains.

Key Features

  • Real-time visibility across all supply chain touchpoints
  • Intelligent alerting with risk-based prioritization
  • Automated response workflows and intervention triggers
  • Performance dashboards with predictive analytics
  • Multi-tier supplier and logistics partner integration
